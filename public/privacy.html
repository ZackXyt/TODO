凝光 · Lumen 隐私政策

最近更新：2026 年 4 月 28 日

这是一个由 UCI 学生 Yiting Xu（Zack）独立开发的免费任务管理 PWA。我们尽可能不收集你的数据，下面把我们做的事讲清楚。

我们收集什么

  • 邮箱地址：登录账号用，由 Firebase Authentication 处理。我们不会用它发广告。
  • 你创建的内容：任务、清单、随想录、专注次数等——存于 Google Firestore，仅你本人可读写（受 Firestore 安全规则保护）。
  • 设备信息：当前登录设备的型号粗略名称（如「iPhone」「Mac · Chrome」）+ 一个本地生成的 UUID + 最后活跃时间。用于多设备同步显示「你在几台设备登录」。
  • 聚合活跃度：作为创作者，我可能查看 聚合统计（DAU / WAU / 设备分布），用于判断功能是否被用、是否值得继续维护。

我们 不 收集什么

  • ❌ 你的 IP 地址（不主动收集；Firebase 服务端日志可能临时记录用于反滥用，但我们看不到）
  • ❌ 你的精确地理位置（天气模块用的城市是你自己输入或浏览器粗略 geolocation）
  • ❌ 任何浏览历史、击键内容
  • ❌ 任何第三方追踪 cookie / 广告 SDK / 统计 SDK

谁能看到你的数据

  • 你自己：所有数据原文。
  • 创作者（我）：作为 admin 我可以查看 所有用户的数据用于聚合分析。我承诺不会在公开场合披露任何个人内容、不会卖给任何人、不会用作个人用途之外的事。如有需要引用某段话作为产品反馈案例，我会脱敏处理。
  • Google：作为 Firebase 服务商，承担数据存储职责，受其自身隐私政策约束。

你的权利

  • 访问：在扩展坞的「📤 导出备份」可一键下载你的全部本地数据。
  • 删除：在扩展坞「账号」区有「删除账号」按钮，会永久清除云端 users/{你的uid}/* 下的全部数据。
  • 撤回同意：随时退出登录即可——本地数据保留，不再上云。

数据保留

账号删除后立即销毁；只要账号存在就保留（这是同步服务的必要前提）。

儿童隐私

本应用不面向 13 岁以下儿童。如发现误注册的未成年账号请联系我删除。

Lumen Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 28, 2026

Lumen is a free task-management PWA built independently by Yiting Xu (Zack), a student at UC Irvine. We collect as little as we can. Here's what we do.

What we collect

  • Email address: for authentication, handled by Firebase Auth. We never use it for marketing.
  • Content you create: tasks, lists, notepad, focus stats — stored in Google Firestore, readable/writable only by you (enforced by Firestore security rules).
  • Device info: a coarse device label ("iPhone", "Mac · Chrome"), a locally generated UUID, and last-active timestamp — used to show your logged-in devices across the app.
  • Aggregate activity: as the creator I may inspect aggregate stats (DAU / WAU / device distribution) to inform product decisions.

What we do not collect

  • ❌ Your IP address (not actively collected; Firebase backend may log briefly for abuse prevention, beyond our reach)
  • ❌ Precise geolocation (the weather module uses a city you type, or coarse browser geolocation you opt into)
  • ❌ Browsing history, keystroke content
  • ❌ Any third-party tracking cookies / ad SDK / analytics SDK

Who can see your data

  • You: all your data in plaintext.
  • The creator (me): as admin I can read all user data for aggregate analysis. I commit to never publicly disclose personal content, never sell it, and never use it beyond product improvement. If I quote a snippet as feedback, I will redact identifying details.
  • Google: as the Firebase provider, responsible for storage, governed by its own privacy policy.

Your rights

  • Access: the dock's "📤 Export Backup" downloads all your local data as JSON.
  • Deletion: the dock's "Account" section has a "Delete account" button; this permanently removes everything under users/{your-uid}/* in the cloud.
  • Withdraw consent: log out at any time — local data is preserved but won't sync to the cloud anymore.

Data retention

Wiped immediately on account deletion; retained for as long as your account exists (necessary for the sync service).

Children's privacy

Lumen is not directed at children under 13. Please contact me to delete any underage account that may have been registered.
